1.Don't Buy Instagram Followers 

	When it comes to growth on Instagram — unless Taylor Swift has mentioned you 
	in a song — it doesn’t happen overnight.

But shortcuts like buying followers or bots aren’t the answer.

Buying followers is against Instagram’s Terms of Use, and while you might 
see an initial surge in followers and engagement, it’ll do you a 
disservice in the long-run. 

Why? Because Instagram frequently weeds out phony engagement and fake 
accounts — and you could find yourself shadowbanned.

It might take some time, but attracting quality followers who’ll 
actually engage with your content and stick around is worth the wait.
